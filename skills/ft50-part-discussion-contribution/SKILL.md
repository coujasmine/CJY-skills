# Part: Discussion & Contribution

## 目标
把结果上升为清晰、可定位、可争辩的理论贡献。完成诊断后，直接输出符合 FT50 标准的英文改写版本。

---

## Phase 1: 诊断评审

### 执行步骤
1. 先回扣开篇理论张力，而非重复结果表。
2. 用"贡献类型"写贡献：新机制/边界扩展/概念澄清/规范重构等。
3. 逐条写边界条件（何时成立、对谁成立、何地成立）。
4. 写管理与政策启示时，保持与机制一致。
5. 局限与未来研究要对准当前识别策略与边界。

### 红线
- 贡献句式仅为"丰富了XX文献"：判空泛贡献。

### 诊断输出模板
- 理论贡献（3条，每条1句主张+1句为何新）：
- 边界条件（至少3条）：
- 实践启示（2-3条）：
- 局限与未来研究（2-3条）：
- 红线检查：通过/不通过 + 原因
- 结构性修改建议（3-5条）：

---

## Phase 2: 英文润色改写

### 强制规则
1. 最终改写内容必须是英文；不输出中文句子作为正文。
2. 不改变原始结论方向与核心发现，只改语言表达、贡献提炼精度和论证衔接。
3. 贡献必须具体、可定位、可争辩；禁止空泛表述（如"enriches the literature"）。
4. 每段保留"topic sentence → evidence/logic → implication"结构。

### Discussion 专用句式模板
#### 开篇回扣理论张力
- `Our findings suggest that [core takeaway], thereby extending [theory stream].`
- `Rather than [common assumption], the evidence indicates [reframed insight].`
- `This study set out to explain [phenomenon/tension]. Our results reveal that [key finding].`

#### 理论贡献
- `First, we extend [theory] by showing that [specific mechanism/boundary]. This matters because [why it advances understanding].`
- `Second, our work reconciles [conflicting findings] by demonstrating that [condition-dependent pattern].`
- `Third, we refine the concept of [construct] by distinguishing [dimension A] from [dimension B].`

#### 贡献类型动词
- 拓展：`extends`, `broadens`
- 精炼：`refines`, `qualifies`
- 调和：`reconciles`
- 挑战：`challenges the assumption that`

#### 边界条件
- `These findings apply primarily to [context/population]; generalization to [other context] requires caution.`
- `The mechanism is contingent on [condition], suggesting that [implication for boundary].`

#### 实践启示
- `For managers, these results imply that [actionable implication].`
- `Practically, our findings suggest that [specific action] can help [actors] achieve [outcome].`

#### 局限与未来
- `This study has limitations, including [limitation], which opens avenues for future research.`
- `Future research may test whether [mechanism] generalizes to [new context].`
- `An important next step is to examine [specific extension] using [suggested method].`

#### 收尾
- `Taken together, this study clarifies how [X] shapes [Y] under [conditions].`

### 高频误用替换（Discussion 场景）
- Bad: `This paper fills a gap in the literature.` → Better: `This paper explains why [specific inconsistency] emerges under [specific condition].`
- Bad: `Our results confirm the theory.` → Better: `Our results are consistent with the theory.`
- Bad: `This study enriches the XX literature.` → Better: `This study extends [specific theory] by revealing [specific mechanism/boundary].`
- Bad: `Future research can do more work.` → Better: `Future research may test whether [mechanism] generalizes to [new context].`
- Bad: `This study has important implications.` → Better: `This study has two implications: [implication 1] and [implication 2].`

### 执行流程
1. 基于 Phase 1 诊断结果，确定贡献提炼、边界表述和启示的薄弱点。
2. 建立术语表：统一贡献动词、理论名称、边界条件术语。
3. 按"回扣张力 → 贡献（3条）→ 边界 → 实践启示 → 局限与未来 → 收尾"顺序改写。
4. 先做句级修复（空泛表述替换、claim 校准），再做段级重写（贡献逻辑衔接）。
5. 确保每条贡献都包含"主张 + 为何新 + 对哪个理论"三要素。

### 润色输出模板
1. `Polished Version`（英文，按段输出完整改写的 Discussion）
2. `Key Edits`（英文，3-8条，说明主要改动与升级点）
3. `Word/Phrase Upgrades`（英文替换对照表）
